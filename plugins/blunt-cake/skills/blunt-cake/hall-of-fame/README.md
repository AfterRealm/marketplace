# Hall of Fame (& Shame)

The community leaderboard for Blunt Cake roasts. Submit your best (or worst) scores.

## How to Submit

1. Run Blunt Cake on your code
2. Generate a shareable roast card (type "card" after the roast)
3. Open a PR to this repo adding your entry to the appropriate board below
4. Include: your roast card, the score, and the language/framework

**Or** post in [GitHub Discussions](../../discussions) with the `hall-of-fame` tag.

## Submission Rules

1. **No fake scores.** Submissions must include the full roast output (in a collapsible `<details>` block).
2. **Anonymize if you want.** You can redact file names and variable names — the score and findings matter, not your codebase.
3. **One entry per person per board.** Update your entry if you beat your own record.
4. **Be a good sport.** The Hall of Shame is self-roasting, not code-shaming others.
5. **Include the personality.** Tell us which language pack you used — Snoop Dogg roasts hit different.
